Care Leavers

Rarer than a millionaire.

A person in the UK is likelier to become a millionaire than a care leaver is to reach a top university. Built from public DfE and UCAS data as an interactive scrollytelling piece, this is a personal project and a personal story: more care leavers reach university than ever, yet the higher you look, the wider the gap grows, until it becomes a chasm.
